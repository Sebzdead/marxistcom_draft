Are you ready to fight?
Millions can see that the world is on fire and that revolutionary upheavals are being prepared on a world scale. In the midst of this crisis of the global capitalist system, the only force capable of transforming society is the organized and mobilized working class. But in order for our class to fight and win, we need to build an international organization to coordinate this struggle.

The Revolutionary Communist International has made outstanding progress in the recent period, growing by leaps and bounds. However, the pace of history is accelerating and we urgently need to expand our forces.

"The world political situation as a whole is chiefly characterized by a historical crisis of the leadership of the proletariat." - Leon Trotsky

"Without revolutionary theory there can be no revolutionary movement." - V.I. Lenin

The building of revolutionary organizations around the world is the burning question of our time; not in the remote future, but now. We are determined to carry out this historic task. But in order to achieve it, we need you. The website below lists some of our national sections, but there are also groups of comrades building in many more countries. If you do not see your country listed, please reach out to us via the form!
